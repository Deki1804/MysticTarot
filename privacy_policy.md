# Privacy Policy for Mystic Tarot AI

**Last Updated: February 2026**

Mystic Tarot AI ("we," "our," or "us") provides a mobile application for entertainment purposes. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## 1. Information We Collect
*   **Firebase Authentication**: If you sign in via Google, we receive your email and name to manage your account.
*   **Firestore**: We store your "Journal" (reading history) to provide the app's core features.
*   **Analytics**: We use Firebase Analytics to track app usage (e.g., how many readings are performed) to improve the experience.

## 2. Third-Party Services
We use the following services which may collect data according to their own policies:
*   **Google Play Services**: Core Android functionality.
*   **Firebase**: Authentication, Database, and Analytics.
*   **Google Gemini (Vertex AI)**: Used to generate tarot readings. No personal identifier data is sent to the AI service.
*   **AdMob**: We display ads to provide free readings. AdMob may use device identifiers for personalized ads.
*   **RevenueCat**: Used to manage in-app purchases and subscriptions.

## 3. Data Storage
Your data is stored securely using Google Cloud infrastructure (Firebase). You can delete your account or specific journal entries at any time within the app.

## 4. Disclaimer (Entertainment Only)
All readings provided by Mystic Tarot AI are generated by Artificial Intelligence. They are for **entertainment purposes only** and should not be used as medical, legal, financial, or professional advice.
